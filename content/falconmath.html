Falcon Math

About

Falcon Math is a week-long summer enrichment program designed for students in grades 4–8 who want to boost their mathematical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity through engaging lessons and competitions.

Students will explore concepts in number theory, logic, algebra, geometry, and more—all taught in an intuitive and challenge-based way. Our experienced instructors work closely with participants to build skills and confidence while having fun.

Each day includes instructional sessions, group activities, and games, culminating in a friendly math competition at the end of the week. Whether you're looking to get ahead or just love math, Falcon Math has something for everyone!
